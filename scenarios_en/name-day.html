Birthday

Ann and her mum are thinking about organising a surprise party for Ann's dad. Ann says: "Mum, if we throw a surprise party for dad on Saturday, we will give him a great joy. The only sad person will be aunt Lusia who can't come on Saturday." Mum replies: "I don't care whether it makes her sad. I just want to make your dad happy. We are throwing a party on Saturday." The birthday party was organised on Saturday. As expected, the aunt felt bad she couldn't come.

Did Ann's mum deliberately hurt aunt Lusia?
